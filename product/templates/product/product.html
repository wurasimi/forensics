Our vision

To become a world-class Forensic Institution recognized for our pioneering research, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to excellence in the field. We aspire to foster a culture of innovation and collaboration, empowering our students and faculty to push the boundaries of forensic science. Through our dedication to integrity, ethics, and the pursuit of truth, we aim to shape the future of forensic investigation and make a lasting impact on society.

Our mission

To advance knowledge and skills in Forensic Investigation and Criminal Intelligence, addressing the evolving demands of justice. Through comprehensive education and research, we empower individuals with the expertise needed to excel in their roles. Collaborating with law enforcement and legal entities, we drive innovation and progress in forensic science. Upholding the highest standards of integrity and ethics, we contribute to a more just and secure society.

Our core value

Excellence in Forensics is our Hallmark. We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of quality, precision, and professionalism in all aspects of our work. Through relentless pursuit of excellence, we strive to set the benchmark for forensic practice and education. Our commitment to excellence serves as the foundation of our institution, guiding our actions and decisions every step of the way. It is not just a goal but a fundamental principle that defines who we are.

ABOUT IAF

The International Academy of Forensics (IAF) is an independent organisation instituted to provide innovative research and development, and world-class professional career development in Forensic Investigation and Criminal Intelligence, with application to the administration of justice. Globally, the Academy collaborates with high impact institutions to offer specialised training programmes in the field of knowledge. We equip our members with ranges of advanced knowledge, skills and tools to unravel real-life crime problems and resolve lingering uncertainties in the legal system.
